Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply navigate to the Courses page, select your desired course, and click the "Enroll Now" or "View Course" button. You may need to log in or create an account first.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and sometimes offer local payment options depending on your region. Check the course enrollment page for details.

Can I get a refund for a course?

Refund policies vary by course provider and subscription type. Generally, you can request a refund within 14 days of purchase if you have not completed a significant portion of the course. Please refer to our Terms of Service for specific conditions.

How do scholarships work?

Our scholarship portal allows you to search and apply for various scholarships. Each scholarship has specific eligibility criteria and application deadlines. We recommend reviewing each scholarship's details carefully and submitting all required documents on time.

How do I receive my certification?

Upon successful completion of all course modules and passing any required assessments, your certification will be automatically added to your "My Certifications" section. You can then download or share your certificate digitally.

I'm having technical issues with the platform. What should I do?

First, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. If the issue persists, please contact our technical support team using the form below or via email, providing as much detail as possible about the problem.
